CyberSecurity

I learned about hackers and the many ways they can hack. First of there are two types of hackers: White Hat (good hackers) & Black Hat (bad hackers). Black Hats usually try to get personal information about people whereas White Hats test the security of a website. There are also different kinds of cyber attacks. There are DDoS attacks and Phishing. DDoS is when they shut down a server by over loading it. Phsishing is basically when they catfish someone into giving private information about themselves throrugh a unsecure website. These are usually done by emails and messages.
